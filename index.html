Welcome to our 2D version of Minecraft. In our game, you will be given some tools that enable you to gather specific types of tiles. You will then be able to put the tiles you have gathered anywhere in the map where there isn't already a tile. The map is generated randomly at each session, for more fun... enjoy :)
